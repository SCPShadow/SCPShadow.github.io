Interesting facts about Maine Coons
1. The Maine Coon is the official state cat of the US state of Maine and has been recognised as such since 1985 (only 3 other US states recognise cats as their state symbol) 2. In the Harry Potter films, all the tricks needed for the filming were split between 6 different Maine Coon cats playing Mrs. Norris! 3. They can be up to 8kg heavier than the average domestic cat. 4. The longest registered cat was 48.5 inches long and was, of course, a Maine Coon named Stewie. 5. Maine Coon cats love water! While most other domestic cats may not care too much for water, a Maine Coon will quite happily jump into your bath!
