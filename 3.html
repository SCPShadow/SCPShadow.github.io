Interesting facts about Ragdolls
1. The breed name Ragdoll was given because of the cat having a tendency to go limp when lifted, just like a rag doll. But as relaxed as these cats may be when being handled. 2. When Ragdolls are born, they are pure white and develop their coat colour and pattern over the first 2 weeks of their life. 3. Their fur colour is only activated in parts of the body that have the coolest temperature. 4. Ragdolls have tufts of hair between their toes, like other long hair cats, this is to protect them from direct contact with wet and icy ground. 5. Just like dogs, a Ragdoll will often greet you when you come home!
